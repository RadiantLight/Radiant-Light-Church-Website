Radiant Light Nursery Ministry and Radiant Light Kids FAQ's

What if my child needs me?

If your child needs you for any reason while you are participating in the service, a team member will be sent to come get you.


What time can I check-in my child(ren)?

For first time guests, an information form about your child will be given for you to fill out. information such as allergies, any special needs your child has will be on the form. Parents are strongly encouraged to complete the check-in process prior to the start of each service. Nurseries and classes are open 15 minutes before the service is slated to begin.


Can someone else pick up my child(ren)?

For safety purposes, each child is provided with a unique check-in number and is only released from the classroom when a parent or responsible guardian provides the corresponding tag. Should you accidentally misplace the tag, the room leader will ask you a few additional questions to assure your identity. We will NOT dismiss children with an older sibling.


Please alert us of any special needs your child may have.

Special needs involves anything related to development, allergies or behavior. This will help us provide the specific care and attention children may need to make their experience positive.


Sick Policy

For the health and welfare of the other children, we ask that if your child is experiencing any of these symptoms, please not check them in to their class.

They include:
*Fever within 24 hours leading up to the service
*Runny nose not related to allergies
*Rashes/Open wounds
*Productive Cough
*Diarrhea or Vomiting within 24 hours leading up to the service
*Impetigo
*Active Chicken Pox, Measles, Mumps
*Conjunctivitis (pink eye)
*Mouth Sores
*Tuberculosis
*Head Lice/Scabies
*Hepatitis A Virus
*Whooping Cough
